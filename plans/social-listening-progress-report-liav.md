## What We Did

We ran a structured lead qualification process using a social listening platform to find the right **people** at solar EPC companies who would buy Civ Robotics' autonomous layout robots. This is the outbound sales intelligence side — complementing our existing ISO queue pipeline (which finds **projects**, not people).

1. Set up the platform with Civ Robotics-specific personas (VP Ops, Construction Managers, etc.)
2. Configured keywords targeting solar EPCs, competitors (Dusty, Built Robotics, Charge), and industry terms
3. Ran an AI agent through 122 leads across 60+ companies
4. Triaged, scored, and deep-dived on every company

## Key Findings

### Buying Signals That Matter Most

1. **Actively hiring surveyors** = they're spending money on the exact thing we replace
   - SOLV, Blattner, MasTec, Strata, Signal Energy, Mortenson all have active postings

2. **Already using construction robotics** = proven tech buyer, shorter sales cycle
   - Mortenson (CivDot + Built Robotics)
   - Blattner (Built Robotics + Sarcos)
   - Rosendin (Built Robotics + building their own robots)

3. **Massive backlogs** = can't hire surveyors fast enough
   - SOLV: $8B backlog
   - Blattner/Quanta: largest renewable contractor in US
   - Mortenson: 7+ GW executed

### The Automation Stack Is Real

The Tier 1 EPCs are systematically automating construction:

| Step | Manual | Automated | Who's Doing It |
|------|--------|-----------|----------------|
| Vegetation clearing | Crews | Swap Robotics | SOLV |
| **Site layout/staking** | **Survey crews** | **Civ Robotics CivDot** | **Mortenson** |
| Pile driving | Crews | Built Robotics RPD 35 | Blattner, Mortenson, Rosendin |
| Panel installation | Crews | Sarcos / Rosendin proprietary | Blattner, Rosendin |

**Layout is the last major manual bottleneck.** Mortenson is the only EPC that has automated it (with us). Everyone else is still hiring $25-35/hr surveyors.

### Developer vs. EPC Distinction Is Critical

We dropped 10+ "leads" that were actually developers (NextEra, Origis, Pattern, Apex, etc.). They own projects but hire EPCs to build them. They're not our buyers. However, mapping which developers hire which EPCs is valuable:
- NextEra hires SOLV, Blattner, Moss
- Pattern hired Blattner for SunZia
- Apex uses MasTec

This means a single EPC relationship can unlock dozens of projects across multiple developers.
