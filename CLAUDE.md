# CLAUDE.md

## Communication Style

- Always include a "Plain English" section that breaks down technical decisions in simple language, as if explaining to a non-technical CEO.
- Never make edits beyond quick fixes without Fisher's explicit approval. For anything larger, explain the proposed change and wait for a go-ahead.

## Design System

- **All frontend work must follow `frontend/DESIGN.md`** — read it before building or modifying any component.
- Dark warm foundation, Lora (serif) + Geist (sans) editorial typography, amber accent, no blue anywhere.
- Harvey AI is the design reference. When in doubt, choose restraint over decoration.
